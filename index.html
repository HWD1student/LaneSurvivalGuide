Introduction

Hello there, welcome to Lane Tech! Chances are, you probaby weren't taught all that much about what high school would actually be like (only that it's going to be so much harder than middle school, we swear), let alone a selective enrollment high school like Lane, and how it really works. This website is designed to introduce new incoming freshmen to Lane, as well as to give some handy tips that people wish they knew before coming here. Think of this website as a supplemental to other reasources such as Lane Tech's official website, orientation, and the student planner. Happy reading and hope the next four years are as good as they can get! Have fun!

- The "team" (just kidding this is a solo project)
